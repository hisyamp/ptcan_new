CAKRAWALA ADIDAYA NUSANTARA

CAKRAWALA ADIDAYA NUSANTARA (PT. CAN) merupakan perusahan yang berdiri pada tahun 2020 dan bergerak pada bidang usaha perdagangan (jual-beli) energi biomass diantaranya cangkang kelapa sawit (Palm Kernell Shell) dan pellet limbah kayu. Selain itu, PT CAN juga menyediakan produk limbah kelapa sawit lain seperti Bungkil Kelapa Sawit yang biasa digunakan sebagai konsentrat pakan ternak.

2019

Company Established

PKS acquired surrounding Kotawaringin Barat, Central Kalimantan. Start to exploring Palm Oil Mills (POMs) in Central Kalimantan.

2019-2020

Export to the International Markets

In collaboration with MAHODENK INDUSTRIES (PMK), PT CAN be a sub- supplier of PT. Garuda Biomass Indonesia (GBI), begin supplying PKS to customers in abroad (Thailand & Japan).

2021-Now

Begin supplying PKS to the end-user in Domestic Market

by using containers (in partnership with Meratus Line) 70 - 100 containers have been sent monthly from 3 big ports ( Banjarmasin, Sampit & Kumai) to the Java Markets.

Now

Export PKS to European Market

by using containers 50 containers have been sent from Surabaya to European's Market.
